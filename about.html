About Deckline

Sydney's Decking Specialists Since 2009

Building Premium Decks for Sydney Families

Deckline Constructions was founded with a simple mission: to build the best decks in Sydney. Based in the Sutherland Shire, we're a family-owned business that takes immense pride in transforming backyards into beautiful outdoor living spaces.

With over 15 years of experience and 500+ decks built, we've earned a reputation for quality craftsmanship, premium materials, and reliable service. We know Sydney's climate, local council requirements, and what Australian homeowners want from their outdoor spaces.
